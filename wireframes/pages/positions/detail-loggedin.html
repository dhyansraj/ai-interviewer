The Investment Manager Services Division (IMS) at S Corp. is growing rapidly and is seeking new members on our Centralized Operations team. The Centralized Operations team is responsible for servicing existing Hedge Fund clients and assisting with the conversion and implementation of future business. The Account Manager is responsible for all aspects of the relationship including supporting S Corp.'s proprietary workflow system, the portfolio accounting system (Geneva), and supplemental systems/technology. This individual must have excellent verbal and written communication skills and be comfortable explaining processes both over the phone and in face to face meetings with clients. Ideally the candidate would be proficient with Microsoft Excel & Access and be comfortable working/manipulating data. The candidate is also required to have a thorough understanding of processes and procedures of a Hedge Fund's Operations, and be able to discuss our service offering in detail.

What you will do:

  • You will serve as main daily client contact, cultivating and maintaining close relationships with clients through proactive measures and written/verbal communication.
  • You will serve as main daily client contact.

What we need from you:

  • BS/BA degree in accounting, finance, business or related field, or the equivalent in education and work experience.
  • Minimum 2 years of related industry/work experience.
  • Strong analytical, problem-solving, organizational, communication and meticulous attention to detail.
  • Intermediate skills in Microsoft Excel.
  • The self-motivation, organization and drive to complete multiple client deliverables in a timely manner without sacrificing excellence or quality.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills as you will primarily support your clients through email and/or phone calls.
  • Strong customer service skills as you will be communicating daily with your clients and other service providers.

What we would like from you:

  • A drive to broaden one's knowledge of the financial services industry by learning new concepts and systems and taking the initiative to apply it to daily work assignments.
